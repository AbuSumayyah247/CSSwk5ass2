A brief explanation on the difference between paddings, margins, and borders. PADDING: Padding is the space between the edge of a content and the wall of a content border. MARGIN: is the space between the outter part of a border line and box of another content BORDER: is the outline that serve as a container for a content. Border is found between padding and margin spaces.
